Hello everyone! My name is Arianna and my business partner is Natalia. Here's a little bit about us and our business. We both have a love for candles, so we decided we were going to make a business out of it ! Our business has been up and running for around a year now. Our candles are 100% soy wax and cruelty free. We also hand pick and purchase our crystals individually so each one is special. We love what we do, and put so much time and effort into our candles. However, since we are a small business, we haven't been able to reach as many people as we had hoped to. We want people to enjoy our candles as much as we do. As well as hear what our customers would like to see more of. We are always looking for new ideas and ways to improve our candles. We thank each and every one of you for visiting our shop, and hope you would support our small business by following us on our socials or purchasing one or two of our yummy smelling candles !
